Personnel Section

8988-4242 loc. 8312emma.flores@quezoncity.gov.ph
  1. Prepares appointment papers of personnel;
  2. Safekeeps all 201 files, including the Personal Data Sheets of the members of the Sanggunian;
  3. Keeps records of attendance;
  4. Prepares application for leave and other papers relating to personnel matters;
    • Compute leave of absences and under time (earned leave)
    •  Safe keep files of leave of absences
    • Prepares monthly report of absences
    • Prepares applications for GSIS loans, Pag-ibig, Kapamalq, Medicare and Dividends of Personnel
  5. Recommends measures designed to upgrade personnel efficiency and promoted sound personnel management;
    • Position classification/reclassification
    • Performance rating (twice a year)
    • Qualification standards
  6. Prepares certification pertaining to official personnel records;
    • Record of employment
    • Salary received
  7. Prepares notice of salary adjustment/ increases
  8. Prepares monthly / quarterly/ annual reports and statistics of personnel;
    • GSIS
    • Leave of absence
  9. Prepares service records of personnel
  10. Prepares resignation, retirement, transfer, separations, reinstatements and terminal leave of personnel
  11. Custodian of following;
    • Statement of Assets Liabilities & Net Worth (SALN)
    • 201 file
    • Various office forms pertaining to personnel;

Ordinance and Resolution Section

8988-4242 loc. 8314arvin.padual@quezoncity.gov.ph
  1. Organizes drafts of proposed ordinances and resolutions;

  1. Keeps a record of all ordinances and resolutions approved by the City Council, including their supporting materials and papers;
