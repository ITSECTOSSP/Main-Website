Sections

Records and Correspondence Section

  1. Records communications received by the office;
  2. Files in a systematic manner all records and take charge in their safekeeping;
  3. Records in a separate book kept for the purpose all ordinances and resolutions filed by each councilors;
  4. Prepares correspondence and other communication;
  5. Prepares certification pertaining to official records;
  6. Release all outgoing communication.
  7. Classifies files and keeps all records in the active and current file of the office;
  8. Digital document tracking
  9. Prepares monthly/ annual reports.
^v

Personnel Section

  1. Prepares appointment papers of personnel;
  2. Safekeeps all 201 files, including the Personal Data Sheets of the members of the Sanggunian;
  3. Keeps records of attendance;
  4. Prepares application for leave and other papers relating to personnel matters;
    • Compute leave of absences and under time (earned leave)
    •  Safe keep files of leave of absences
    • Prepares monthly report of absences
    • Prepares applications for GSIS loans, Pag-ibig, Kapamalq, Medicare and Dividends of Personnel
  5. Recommends measures designed to upgrade personnel efficiency and promoted sound personnel management;
    • Position classification/reclassification
    • Performance rating (twice a year)
    • Qualification standards
  6. Prepares certification pertaining to official personnel records;
    • Record of employment
    • Salary received
  7. Prepares notice of salary adjustment/ increases
  8. Prepares monthly / quarterly/ annual reports and statistics of personnel;
    • GSIS
    • Leave of absence
  9. Prepares service records of personnel
  10. Prepares resignation, retirement, transfer, separations, reinstatements and terminal leave of personnel
  11. Custodian of following;
    • Statement of Assets Liabilities & Net Worth (SALN)
    • 201 file
    • Various office forms pertaining to personnel;
^v

Finance and Accounting Section

  1. Determine the supplies and equipment needs of the office;
  2. Keeps a running inventory of supplies and materials including equipment;
  3. Prepares requisition papers for supplies and equipment in advance of the actual need therefor;
  4. Takes charges in the proper maintenance and management of all the supplies and other property of the office;
  5. Provides utility services to the office;
  6. Takes charges in the cleanliness and upkeep of the office premises.
  7. Performs other duties that may be assigned by the higher authorities;
^v

General Services Section

  1. Prepares Attendance Reports, vouchers and its supporting documents relative to salary withdrawals of personnel of the Office of the City Secretary and likewise files copies of the same;
  2.  Indexing/carding of personnel’s salaries, allowances and benefits, including GSIS/Pag-IBIG loans, Kapamalq deposits, etc., based from the issued payships and special Payrolls prepared by the HRMD
  3. Records all disbursement of funds, keeps up-to-date balance of funds pertaining to the Office appropriations and prepares quarterly requests for allotments;
  4. Prepared Budget Proposal of the office every succeeding year;
  5.  Prepares necessary communications related to accounting and other financial matters;
^v

Sections

Journal and Transcript Section

  1. Takes stenographic notes of the proceedings of the City Council during its regular or special sessions;
  2. Transcribes such stenographic notes;
  3. Keeps records of the journal of the proceedings of the sessions of the City Council;
  4. Performs such other work as may be assigned.
^v

Committee Affairs Section

  1. Coordinates with the various committee of the City Council in setting the date, time and venue of the committee meetings;
  2. Takes notes of the proceedings of the meetings of the various committees of the City Council and prepares the minutes and journals thereof;
  3. Keeps records of all the minutes and journals of the meetings of the committees of the City Council;
^v

Minutes Section

  1. Takes notes of the proceedings of the City Council during its regular or special session;
  2. Prepares the minutes of the regular or special session of the City Council;
  3. Keeps records of the minutes of all the regular or special sessions of the City Council;
  4. Performs such other works as may be assigned by the higher supervisors.
^v

Special Committee

  1. Receive Barangay Legislative Measures Files/Referred by the City Council for review.
  2. Prepares Agenda for SRCBO and distribute copy of the same to the members of the committee and the reserve for the meeting.
  3. Records the proceedings and prepares minutes of meeting.
^v

Sections

Ordinance and Resolution Section

  1. Organizes drafts of proposed ordinances and resolutions;
  2. Finalizes and reproduces approved ordinances and resolutions of the City Council for 3rd and final reading;
  3.  Prepares final draft of approved ordinances and resolutions by the City Council;
  4. Forwards enacted ordinances and adopted resolutions for the signature of the Preceding Officer/Vice Mayor and the City Mayor.
  5. Verifies ordinances and resolutions whether signed by the proper authorities;
  6. Prepares the reproduction of approved resolutions and ordinances for proper dissemination;
  7. Maintain records of all the materials of approved ordinances and resolutions;
^v

Agenda and Briefing Section

  1. Prepares agenda of regular and special sessions of the City Council;
  2.  Prepares referrals to various City Council Commitees, such as: proposed measures, barangay budgets; excerpts.
  3. Safekeeps supporting materials and documents;
  4.  Reproduces copies of supporting materials/documents.
  5.  Prepares referrals for Barangays’ Budget.
^v

Legislative Research Section

  1.  Undertake researches and studies on materials needed in the drafting of ordinances and resolutions;
  2. Reviews proposed ordinances and resolutions for the purposes of determining possible conflicts with existing ordinances and resolutions or duplication therewith pursuant to Resolution no. SP-1028, S-98;
  3. Makes a comprehensive study of all existing ordinances and resolutions for purposes of classification and compilation;
  4. Other duties as may be designated by the City Secretary.
^v

Reference and Archives Section

  1. Keeps a record of all ordinances and resolutions approved by the City Council, including their supporting materials and papers;
  2.  Prepares transmittal to the City Mayor and other offices concerned copies of duly approved ordinances and resolutions;
  3. Catalogs and indexes all approved ordinances and resolutions for purposes of ready reference;
  4. Records in a bookkept for purpose the names of individual councilors showing all approved ordinances and resolutions which they introduced.
  5. Verifies ordinances and resolutions whether signed by the proper authorities;
^v
