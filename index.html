About Us

Provides competent, quality, adequate and efficient administrative, secretarial, legislative and technical support services to the Sangguniang Panlungsod in the performance of its functions as the legislative, deliberative and policy making body of the City Government

Certify Documents

Certificate of True Copy of Quezon City Council approved Ordinance, Resolutions, Legislative Documents, and Records on File, Issuances of Certificate of Appearance.

Legislative Records

Repository of legislative records and documents officially acted upon by the Quezon City Council including approved Ordinances, Resolutions, materials and annexes forming integral parts thereof, including records of public records

Provide Information

Post and Publishes information of approved Ordinances and its penalties, Furnishes copy of approved Ordinances and Resolutions to concerned Officials of the City, including government officials and private sector.
